Dr. Apj Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Visionary Leader

About

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002-2007). Known for his humility and brilliance, he played a key role in India’s missile development and space programs. He was deeply passionate about inspiring youth to dream big.

Achievements

  • Chief architect of India's missile program (AGNI, PRITHVI)
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Author of inspirational books like "Wings of Fire"
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna (India’s highest civilian award)

Inspirational Quotes

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
"You have to dream before your dreams can come true."
